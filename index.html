Hi! I'm Hsiang-Chih Hwang. I have recently graduated from National Taiwan University with double Bachelor degrees in Physics and Electrical Engineering.

As part of my undergraduate research, I worked on:

  • Numerical modelling to build the relation between magnetic field in stellar jets and the polarization of molecular line.

  • Study of protoplanetary disk formation with ALMA data, in which I looked into the velocity profile of the disk to understand the influence of the magnetic field.

  • Designing and implementing particle-related functions into our code "GAMER" to allow particles to evolve precisely with adaptive mesh and individual time step.

For more information, check Research!
