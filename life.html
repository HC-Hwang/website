Trio Concert

2013/2/23

I studied in a special class for musically-talented students in elementary school and junior high school, majoring in piano and minoring in viola. Although I no longer study music, I still participate in many music activities. In my third year of college, some friends and I decided to arrange a trio concert, aiming to perform the masterpiece: "A La Memoire D'un Grand Artiste (In memory of a great artist)," by Tchaikovsky. It took us one year to prepare, and we had a great time in collaborating and practicing. Now I don't play the piano as much as I used to, but this concert was undoubtedly the best achievement in my career of music.

Badminton Life

2010/9 - 2015/6

I was once the captain of the Electronic Engineering Dept.'s badminton team, and I stayed on as a team member throughout my college. I was first a trainee, and then a trainer among my badminton teammates, and I found that I really enjoyed coaching others and seeing them improve. Even more, in the badminton team I learned how to organize group events and manage its finance. The atmosphere of our team is like a great family; we practiced hard and have won numerous prizes in many competitions together.
