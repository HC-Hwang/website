Hello,
I'm Hsiang-Chih Hwang!

I was born in Taipei, Taiwan. I have always been interested in physics, but it was not until my university that I decided my career of astrophysics. I studied in a music-professioned class before entering senior high school, majoring in piano and minoring in viola. After receiving full marks in College Entrance Exam, I decided to major in Electrical Engineering, the most sought out major at National Taiwan University. Because of my passion for physics, I double-majored in Physics and became very devoted to astronomical research.

I love spreading exciting discovery of the Universe and sharing the scientific story behind the dark sky with people around me, whether to college students in courses I TA or to fellow comrades in my mandatory military service. I enjoy exploring Nature and helping others see the scientific beauty of the world around us.
